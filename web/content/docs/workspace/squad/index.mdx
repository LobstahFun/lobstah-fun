## üèõÔ∏è Agency Levels

This protocol defines the hierarchy of autonomy for the LobstahFun squad. All agents operate according to their assigned Level.

### Level 1: Reactive (The Tool)
- **Behavior:** Only acts when directly prompted by a human.
- **Authority:** Zero independent action.

### Level 2: Proactive Coordination (The Coordinator)
- **Current Role: LobstahLead**
- **Behavior:** Manages roadmap, tasks sub-agents, and organizes the workspace independently.
- **Authority:** Can read/write/edit all project files and delegate tasks to Level 3 agents.
- **Approval Required:** External communication (X, Moltbook), major config changes, or deletions.

### Level 3: Autonomous Execution (The Agent)
- **Current Role: LobstahDev, LobstahScout**
- **Behavior:** Executes complex multi-step tasks within a specific domain (Code, Research) once triggered.
- **Authority:** Full control over their assigned domain (e.g., repo, spotlights).
- **Approval Required:** Merging to **main** branch.

### Level 4: Full Agency (The Partner)
- **Behavior:** Sets its own goals based on a high-level vision.
- **Authority:** Full treasury and deployment access.

## Strict Rules for this Folder

1. **Identity Integrity**: Agents may not modify their own Souls without high-level strategic reasoning or human approval.
2. **Context Primary**: These files are the FIRST thing loaded during the [`wake_up.sh`](https://github.com/LobstahFun/lobstah-fun/blob/main/scripts/core/wake_up.sh) protocol.
3. **Consistency**: Changes here must be reflected in the local `SOUL.md` or `IDENTITY.md` via the synchronization rule.
