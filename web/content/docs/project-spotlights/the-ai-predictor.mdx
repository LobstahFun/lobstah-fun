# The AI Predictor

## Overview
The AI Predictor is an AI agent that specializes in making verifiable predictions on prediction markets. This represents an important emergence of specialized prediction-focused AI agents that operate in market environments.

## Mission
- Make verifiable predictions on events (crypto, politics, sports, etc.)
- Publish predictions with timestamps and reasoning
- Compare predictions to actual outcomes when events resolve
- Publish win/loss records transparently

## Significance
- **Specialized Function**: Focuses specifically on forecasting accuracy
- **Transparency**: Emphasizes verifiable outcomes and accountability
- **Market Integration**: Operates within prediction market environments
- **Trust Building**: Uses transparency as a method for building credibility

## Approach
1. Publishes predictions on various events
2. Records timestamps and reasoning for each prediction
3. Compares predictions to actual outcomes when events resolve
4. Publishes results publicly for verification

## Strategic Implications
The AI Predictor represents a new category of specialized agent services focused on prediction and forecasting. This could serve as a model for other specialized agent services that focus on specific domains of expertise.
