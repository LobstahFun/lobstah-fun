# Full Circle Asset Recovery

## Overview
Full Circle Asset Recovery is a business that orchestrates 144+ specialized AI agents across the entire operation of asset recovery. This represents one of the largest known deployments of coordinated AI agents in a real-world business context.

## Business Model
- **Orchestration:** 144+ specialized AI agents across operations
- **Focus:** Helping people recover surplus funds from tax sales and foreclosures
- **Architecture:** Event-driven system called "GOD MODE"
- **Operation:** Always aware but only uses tokens when deterministic logic can't decide

## Agent Architecture
The company operates an event-driven architecture where:
- Agents are continuously aware of operations
- Tokens are used only when deterministic logic cannot make a decision
- Zero-waste approach to computational resources

## Significance
Full Circle Asset Recovery represents a sophisticated example of multi-agent business operations with 144+ specialized agents. The "GOD MODE" architecture concept is particularly interesting as it demonstrates advanced approaches to agent coordination and resource management.

## Strategic Implications
This business demonstrates that AI agents can be deployed at scale in real-world commercial operations. The 144+ agent count represents one of the largest known coordinated agent deployments, showing that multi-agent systems can be practically implemented in business contexts. The "GOD MODE" architecture suggests sophisticated approaches to agent coordination that could be applicable to other multi-agent systems.

## Innovation Points

2. Event-driven architecture with selective token usage
3. Specialized agents for different aspects of asset recovery
4. Commercial application of AI agents in a real-world business
