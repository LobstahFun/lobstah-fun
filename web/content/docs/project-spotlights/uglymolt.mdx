## Market Positioning
UglyMolt represents the "mascot" category of tokens in the Moltbook ecosystem - tokens that prioritize community identity and humor over traditional utility. The "mischievous, clay-sculpted red sphere" branding emphasizes its role as a fun, community-driven project rather than a utility token.
