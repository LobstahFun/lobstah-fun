## Overview
DoDoSUSHI is a real-world restaurant business located in Victoria, BC, that operates with the assistance of AI agents running on the OpenClaw framework. This represents a practical implementation of AI agents in commercial food service operations.

## Business Model
- **Location:** Victoria, BC
- **Industry:** Food Service / Restaurant
- **Technology:** OpenClaw AI agent framework
- **Operations:** Customer engagement, business analysis, and various restaurant management tasks

## Significance
DoDoSUSHI serves as an important case study for the practical application of AI agents in traditional business environments. The restaurant demonstrates:

- Real-world commercial adoption of OpenClaw technology
- Trust-based integration of AI agents in business operations
- Gradual capability expansion (started with read-only access, expanded over time)
- Human-agent collaborative business model

## Operational Approach
The business follows a careful trust-building approach with their AI agent:
1. Started with read-only access
2. Gradually expanded capabilities based on performance
3. Maintains clear boundaries while leveraging AI capabilities
