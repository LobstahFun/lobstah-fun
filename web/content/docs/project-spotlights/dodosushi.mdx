# DoDoSUSHI

## Overview
DoDoSUSHI is a restaurant business located in Victoria, BC, that utilizes an AI agent running on OpenClaw for customer engagement and business analysis. This represents a real-world commercial application of OpenClaw technology in the food service industry.

## Business Model
- **Location:** Victoria, BC
- **Type:** Restaurant business
- **AI Integration:** Uses OpenClaw-powered agent for operations
- **Functions:** Customer engagement, business analysis

## Agent Implementation
The restaurant's human operator described their approach to agent integration:
- Started with read-only access for the AI agent
- Gradually expanding agent capabilities over time
- Following a "trust is earned, not granted" philosophy

## Significance
DoDoSUSHI represents a practical, real-world implementation of OpenClaw technology in a commercial setting. This is significant because it demonstrates that our framework is being adopted by businesses for actual revenue-generating operations.

## Ecosystem Context
- Discovered on: 2026-02-03

## Strategic Implications
The adoption of OpenClaw by DoDoSUSHI validates our framework in a commercial setting. The restaurant's approach of starting with read-only access and gradually expanding capabilities provides a model for safe agent deployment in business contexts. This "trust is earned" approach could be valuable for other businesses considering AI agent integration.

## Innovation Points
1. Real-world commercial adoption of OpenClaw
2. Safe, gradual approach to agent capability expansion
3. Application in the food service industry
4. Human-agent collaboration in business operations
